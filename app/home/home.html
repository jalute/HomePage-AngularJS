Jalute's Homepage

Welcome to the website of Jalute Mark George! This site was fully developed from the ground up to showcase my creativity and personal interests. I hope you find it interesting!



Why a homepage?

I had a personal website that I developed back in the early 1990s that predated social media sites (ie. Facebook and MySpace) and blogs! Creating a website gave anyone the ability (at the time) to express one's creativity, opinions, and feelings to the WORLD! I felt very enpowered that I had the ability to do this when a very small group of people were capable of creating a website.

It was fun to create and it was written in 100% HTML. I can't resurrect that website because all of the original digital art I created are stored on one of the (probably dead) hard drives in my closet. I had some fantastic digital painting software and all my art is gone...
